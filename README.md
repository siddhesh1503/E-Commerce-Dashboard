# 📊 Ecommerce Sales Dashboard – Power BI

## Overview
This project is an interactive Ecommerce Sales Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze sales performance, profit trends, customer behavior, product categories, and payment methods. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions.

## Objectives
- Track overall sales and profit performance.
- Analyze monthly profit trends.
- Identify top-performing products and customers.
- Understand category-wise sales distribution.
- Evaluate customer payment preferences.
- Enable dynamic filtering by quarter and state.

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI Desktop
- Power Query
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- CSV Data Sources

## Dataset
The dashboard was created using:
- Orders.csv
- Details.csv

## Dashboard Features

### Profit Analysis by Month
- Tracks monthly profit performance.
- Identifies profitable and loss-making months.
- Helps understand seasonal trends.

### Sales by Sub-Category
- Compares revenue generated across product sub-categories.
- Top-performing categories include Printers, Saree, Bookcases, Tables, and Accessories.

### Profit by Sub-Category
- Evaluates profitability across different product groups.
- Helps identify high-margin products.

### Category-wise Quantity Distribution
- Clothing: 63%
- Electronics: 21%
- Furniture: 17%

### Payment Mode Analysis
- COD: 44%
- UPI: 21%
- Debit Card: 13%
- Credit Card: 12%
- EMI: 10%

## Business Insights
- Clothing contributes the highest sales volume.
- COD is the most preferred payment method.
- Printers generate the highest profit among sub-categories.
- Monthly profit fluctuates significantly throughout the year.
- A small group of customers contributes a major share of revenue.
